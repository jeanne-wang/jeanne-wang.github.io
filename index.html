Xiaojuan Wang

I'm a Ph.D student in GRAIL, University of Washington, advised by Steve Seitz, and Brian Curless. I am also closely working with Aleksander Holynski.

Before, I received my Master's degree from ETH Zurich, working with Martin Oswald in CVG lab. I have also spent summer times interning at Meta, Adobe Research, and Google Research.

I am broadly interested in generative AI, especially AI for video content creation. More specifically video and 3D scene synthesis.
