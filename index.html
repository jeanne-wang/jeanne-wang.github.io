Xiaojuan Wang

I'm a fifth-year Ph.D student in GRAIL, University of Washington, advised by Steve Seitz, Brian Curless and Ira Kemelmacher. I also work closely with Aleksander Holynski.

Previously, I received my Master's degree from ETH Zurich, working in the CVG lab. I have also interned at Meta, Adobe Research, and Google Research during summer times.

I am broadly interested in visual generative models, especially video models, and how to use these models to help video content creation in real world applications.
